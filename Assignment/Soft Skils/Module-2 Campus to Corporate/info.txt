 
Module 2: Campus to Corporate 


Task 1: Build your Resume 
• Create a professional resume using a clean, structured format that highlights your skills, education, and achievements. 
• Include the following sections: Personal Info, Objective, Education, Skills, Experience, Projects, Certifications, and Interests. 

Task 2: LinkedIn Profile 
• Create or update your LinkedIn profile to reflect your resume. 
• Ensure your profile picture, headline, and summary are professional and complete all sections: Experience, Skills, and Certifications. 


Submission: 
• Upload the PDF of your resume in your career centre  
• Provide the link to your updated LinkedIn profile in your career centre
